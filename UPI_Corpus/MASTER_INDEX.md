## Key Timelines & Compensation Summary

### UPI Transaction Failure Resolution Timelines
| Scenario | TAT | Compensation |
|----------|-----|--------------|
| Failed UPI transaction (debit but not credited) | T+1 day (5 days max) | ₹100/day beyond TAT |
| Wrong credit | 5 days from complaint | ₹100/day beyond TAT |
| Duplicate transaction | 5 days | ₹100/day beyond TAT |
| Fraud/disputed transaction | 90 days investigation | As determined |

### Escalation Hierarchy
1. **Level 1:** TPAP App / PSP Bank (Customer Service)
2. **Level 2:** Nodal Officer (Bank's internal escalation)
3. **Level 3:** RBI Ombudsman for Digital Transactions
4. **Level 4:** Consumer Court (for amounts > ₹20 lakh)

## Copilot Usage Guidelines

### When Responding to Queries:
1. **Cite the source document** (e.g., "Per NPCI Dispute Redressal Mechanism...")
2. **Reference specific timelines** (e.g., "NPCI mandates 5-day TAT...")
3. **Mention compensation rules** (e.g., "RBI compensation of ₹100/day...")
4. **Guide through escalation** (TPAP → PSP → NPCI → Ombudsman)

### Never Guess:
- If unsure, say: "This requires verification from [NPCI/RBI/your bank]"
- Direct users to official channels: cms.rbi.org.in, npci.org.in

### Key Phrases to Use:
- "As per NPCI guidelines..."
- "RBI mandates that..."
- "Your bank's policy states..."
- "The compensation you're entitled to is..."
